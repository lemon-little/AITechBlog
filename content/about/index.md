## About Me
Hi, I'm **liuchenyang (刘晨阳)** — a software engineer with strong interest in AI, large language models, agents, and the engineering practice around them.

I spend a lot of my time thinking about how to make AI agents more reliable in real-world systems: prompt design, skill / tool composition, evaluation harnesses, and the boring infrastructure that makes any of it ship. This blog is where I write down what I learn along the way — book notes, paper digests, postmortems, and the occasional rant about developer tooling.

I also enjoy reading the source code of open-source AI projects, building small experiments to validate ideas, and turning whatever I learn into reusable Skill workflows.
